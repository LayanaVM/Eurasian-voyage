KOK-tobe

This picturesque spot stands as a serene oasis amidst the bustling city, providing breathtaking panoramic views of the surrounding landscape and the majestic Trans-Ili Alatau mountains. The observation deck and cable car offer sweeping views of Almaty's skyline — a perfect spot for capturing stunning photographs or enjoying the grandeur of nature.

Big Almaty Lake

One of the most stunning natural attractions in Kazakhstan, Big Almaty Lake is famous for its crystal-blue water, peaceful atmosphere, and breathtaking mountain backdrop.
